About me

Long story short...

Creation has always been like magic to me. Knowledge and passion is my special power.

2007 - 2011
University of Świętokrzyskie: IT engineer degree, specialization - ICT.
2011 - 2013
Kielce University of Technology: IT master degree, specialization - graphic design C++.

I wrote my master’s thesis in C++ using the OpenGL library – a virtual 3D world with dynamic weather. Over the years, I’ve developed skills in C, C++, C#, JavaScript, MySQL, HTML, CSS, Python, and more.

My journey with digital design started in 2005. During these years, I created thousands of projects while working for two large printing houses and an IT corporation, and I also launched my own advertising agency with a printing house.
Throughout this time, I gained experience in DTP, Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, 3D, UX/UI, UGC content creation, and many other areas.
And the journey is still going on...
